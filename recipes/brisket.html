Smoked Brisket

Description

Oak smokeked brisket with salt and pepper "dalmation" rub. Made famous in Central Texas.

I don't know what else to write. Look its really good, like my favorite meal kind of good. Just trust me. Stop reading and start cooking.

Ingredients

  • Untrimmed Beef Brisket
  • Kosher Salt
  • 16 Mesh Black Pepper
  • Oak Wood Splits

Steps

  1. Trim brisket leaving a quarter inch of fat.
  2. Mix rub 50 50 salt and pepper.
  3. Evenly coat brisket in rub.
  4. Smoke at 270 degrees for roughly 1 hour per pound.
